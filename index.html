Privacy Policy for Site Summarizer

Site Summarizer does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

  • The extension processes the visible text content of the web page you are viewing in order to generate a summary.
  • No personal, financial, health, or authentication information is collected.
  • No browsing history or user activity is tracked or stored.
  • No data is sold or transferred to third parties.
  • Summaries are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to any server except for the purpose of generating the summary via the HuggingFace API.
  • Only the text to be summarized is sent to the HuggingFace API; no other data is transmitted.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at venkateshe511@gmail.com
